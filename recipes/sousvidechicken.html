Sous Vide Chicken

The Recipe

  1. Fill a pot 1/2 full with water. I'd sugest something at least 8 qt. Preheat your sousvide to 165 F
  2. Season the chicken how you would like. My best results came from using a premade spice packet and cutting the chicken up into reasonable sized pieces.
  3. Vacuum seal the chicken in a gallon bag
  4. Place the bag into the pot and clip it to the sides. Leave it in there for at least 2 hours
  5. Heat a skillet
  6. Cook the chicken on the skillet until brown on both sides. Serve
