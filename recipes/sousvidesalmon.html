Sous Vide Salmon

The Recipe

  1. Fill a pot 1/2 full with water. I'd sugest something at least 8 qt. Preheat your sousvide to 165 F
  2. Season the salmon how you would like
  3. Heat the sous vide to 135
  4. Bag and seal the salmon
  5. Cook for an hour and a half
  6. Remove Salmon and pat dry
  7. Heat on skillet and serve.
